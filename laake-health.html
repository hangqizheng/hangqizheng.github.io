Laake Health: AI autonomous medical coding system for healthcare

Medical coding is traditionally manual and error-prone - we automated that, ensuring speed and accuracy. The challenge is that out-of-the-box LLM doesn't generate reliable, deterministic output. Through combining rule-based decision paths with LLM's chain of thoughts, we break down medical coding into letter-by-letter steps. We have interactive highlights tracing back to the original source, making each coding decision easy to verify.
